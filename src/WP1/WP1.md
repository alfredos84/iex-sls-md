# WP1  

### Bulk glass preparation and baseline characterization (almost done) The goal is to establish reliable structural and mechanical reference for Na₂O–CaO–SiO₂ glasses. 

## Simulations 

- Prepare base composition $$(75)\text{SiO}_2 - (10)\text{CaO} - (15-x)\text{Na}_2\text{O} - (x)\text{K}_2\text{O}$$ via melt–quench.  

- Validate density, coordination numbers (Si–O, Na–O, Ca–O), $$Q^{n}$$ distribution, and ring statistics. 

- Compute elastic properties at 300 K using small-strain deformation. 

- Compute MSD and AE for diffusion 

## Analysis 

- Radial distribution functions and coordination distributions. 

- $$Q^{n}$$ speciation and ring size distributions. 

- Young’s modulus, bulk modulus, shear modulus, Poisson ratio. 

- Atomic virial stress distribution (baseline). 

- Voronoi analysis for free volume? 

### The deliverable will be the validation bulk soda–lime silicate models with mechanical properties and diffusion.
